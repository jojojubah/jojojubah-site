Starter Notes

Supply & Demand (Fast Intuition)

Prices move where buyers and sellers agree. Shocks (news, scarcity) shift those curves. Tip: always ask "what changed the balance?"

Read mini-note

Inflation vs. Deflation

Inflation = rising prices, deflation = falling prices. Moderate inflation motivates spending; deflation can stall it. Why it matters for creators: pricing, contracts, and savings.

Read mini-note

Compounding & Time

Tiny repeated edges beat big one-offs. Audience, skills, and cashflows all compound when you keep the flywheel turning.

Read mini-note

Market Psychology

Fear and greed drive cycles. Understanding crowd behavior helps creators time launches, spot trends, and avoid panic decisions.

Read mini-note

Network Effects

Products become more valuable as more people use them. Think social media, communities, and creator ecosystemsâ€”each new member benefits everyone.

Opportunity Cost

Every choice has a hidden cost: what you gave up. For creators, time spent on one project means less time for another. Always ask: "What am I not doing?"
